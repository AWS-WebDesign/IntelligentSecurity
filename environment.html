environmental call to action

Have you realized that trash polution have created a really negative impact on our life?

Generally, there are three ways to process garbage
  1. landfill
    2. Landfill is the most simple way, but in order to avoide the complex progress of processing the garbage, larger and more developed countries would sell the garbage to smaller and less developed countries to save money.
  2. compost
    3. Compost need a high requirement for the garbage, it need to be sorted and has to contain high organic content. However, the garbage can hardly meet the requirement which means not all the garbage can be processed in this way.
  3. incineration
    4. Relativly speaking, incineration is a better way to process the garbage, but the gas which is produced by incineration is toxic. The toxic gas is called Dioxin and it could cause cancer.

    As we can see, processing garbage is always a complex issue for human and the envrionment. So let's do our part, protect the emvironment through sorting our garbage.
In present days, China is also paying more attention on trash classification.
